Paper Mache Productions

Paper Mache - Four step Production process

From raw material to final production all skillful work done under no mechanization- All our products are environmental friendly

Stage 1 : Preparation of Raw material

Old Newspaper soaked in water for several days until it disintegrates. The excess water is than drained out and soaked waste paper is mixed with rice straw to form pulp.

Stage 2 : Pulp formation and Drying

The mixture is placed in a mould and left it to dry for few days. On the drying of pulp, the shape is cut away from the mould in two halves and then glued again.

Stage 3 : Giving Pulp the shape

The surface is coated with the layer of glue and gypsum, rubbed smooth with a stone or baked piece of clay and pasted with layers of tissue paper which gives it shape.
The shape can be of any form ranging from hanging baubles, to animal shape to normal boxes.
Finally a base color is painted on, and now the item is ready for the artist to grace it with his design.

Stage 4 : Decorating the paper mache pieces

The design work is done by skilled artisan and the paint work is all through hand. The design work is either in the form of flowers, birds or any customised designs.
After the design is complete the final step is adding a layer or more of varnish to give the item its characteristic shine.
